# AgriVora Landing Page - Professional Theme Transformation

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The new theme embodies **class, professionalism, and agricultural excellence** through:

- **Deep Forest Greens**: Sophisticated dark greens (#2D5016) that evoke growth and sustainability
- **Clean White Spaces**: Crisp white backgrounds (#FFFFFF) for maximum clarity and readability
- **Gold Accents**: Subtle golden tones (#B8941F) representing harvest and prosperity
- **Minimal Shadows**: Refined, subtle shadows for depth without distraction
- **Professional Typography**: Clean, readable text hierarchy

## 🏗️ Component Updates

### Navigation Bar
- **Background**: Clean white with 98% opacity
- **Border**: Subtle 1px light gray
- **Shadow**: Minimal (0 1px 3px) for depth
- **Effect**: Professional glassmorphism with 12px blur

### Hero Section
- **Overlay**: Deep forest green gradient (88-92% opacity)
- **Text**: Pure white with text shadows for readability
- **Badges**: White with gold borders and backdrop blur
- **Feel**: Bold, confident, professional

### Cards (Steps, Features, Use Cases)
- **Background**: Pure white
- **Borders**: 1px light gray
- **Shadows**: Subtle (0 1px 3px) at rest

- **Effect**: Clean, modern, professional

### Phone Mockup
- **Frame**: Deep green gradient

### Images

### Final CTA
